Prime Hub V3.6.1 — Custom Wallet Top-up Amount

Adds a "Custom Amount" button to My Wallet > Add Funds.
Customers can enter any amount from $1.00 to $10,000.00 and then choose UPI, Binance Pay, USDT BEP20, or USDT TRC20.

Note: NOWPayments may reject small USDT top-ups because of its dynamic network minimum. UPI and Binance Pay manual top-ups can still use the custom amount.
